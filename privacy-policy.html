Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how PipeMatch may collect, use, store, and manage information when homeowners browse the site, submit a request, or compare local plumbing provider options.

PipeMatch is not a plumbing contractor. Information may be used to support request routing, communication, and platform functionality.

01

Information We Collect

PipeMatch may collect information that users choose to provide through forms, request fields, or communication tools on the site.

  • Name
  • Email address
  • Phone number
  • ZIP code or service area
  • Project timing and service category
  • Details about a plumbing-related request

PipeMatch may also collect technical or usage-related data such as browser type, device information, IP address, referral source, session behavior, and interaction with pages or forms.

02

How We Use Information

Information may be used to support:

  • Website operation and improvement
  • User requests and inquiries
  • Quote request and provider comparison workflows
  • Site analytics, performance, and user experience
  • Security, fraud prevention, and operational reliability
  • Legal obligations where applicable

PipeMatch is not a direct plumbing contractor. Submitted information may be used to support matching or comparison with independent providers.

03

Sharing Information

PipeMatch may share submitted request information with third parties when reasonably necessary to support the platform’s comparison, communication, or request-routing purpose.

  • Service providers supporting site operations or analytics
  • Technology vendors involved in communications or form delivery
  • Independent providers or provider networks connected to the request flow
  • Legal or regulatory parties where disclosure is required by law

PipeMatch does not promise that every request will result in contact, a quote, or a provider match.

Cookies & Tracking

PipeMatch may use cookies, local storage, analytics tools, and similar technologies to support site functionality, remember preferences, measure usage, and improve browsing experience.

Users can review the separate Cookie Policy for more detail about cookie-related tools used on this site.

Data Retention

PipeMatch may retain personal and technical information for as long as reasonably necessary to operate the site, support business purposes, maintain records, resolve disputes, or comply with legal obligations.

Retention periods may vary depending on data type, collection purpose, and applicable legal or operational requirements.

Depending on applicable law, users may have rights related to access, correction, deletion, or limitation of certain personal information.
