Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how information may be collected, used, stored, and managed when users interact with PipeMatch, an independent platform that helps homeowners compare local plumbing service provider options.

Policy note Designed for clarity and transparency.

PipeMatch is a matching platform, not a direct plumbing contractor. This policy describes how data may be handled when users browse the site or submit a request.

Important context

PipeMatch helps users compare local provider options. Information submitted through the site may be used to support that matching and request process.

By using this site, you acknowledge that PipeMatch may collect limited personal and technical information in connection with your browsing activity, request submissions, and communication preferences.

This Privacy Policy is provided for general transparency and should be reviewed together with our Cookie Policy and Terms of Service.

Last updated: April 22, 2026

Information We Collect

PipeMatch may collect information that users choose to provide directly through forms, request fields, or other communication tools on the site.

Examples may include:

  • Name
  • Email address
  • Phone number
  • ZIP code or service area
  • Project timing
  • Details about a plumbing-related request

PipeMatch may also collect certain technical or usage-related data automatically, such as browser type, device information, IP address, referral source, session behavior, and interaction with pages or forms.

How We Use Information

Information may be used for purposes such as:

  • Operating and improving the site
  • Responding to user requests or inquiries
  • Supporting quote request and provider comparison workflows
  • Analyzing site usage, performance, and user experience
  • Maintaining security, fraud prevention, and operational reliability
  • Complying with legal obligations where applicable

PipeMatch is not a direct plumbing contractor. Information submitted through the site may be used to support the matching or comparison process for independent providers.

Sharing Information

PipeMatch may share submitted request information with third parties when reasonably necessary to support the platform’s comparison, communication, or request-routing purpose.

Examples may include:

  • Service providers supporting site operations or analytics
  • Technology vendors involved in communications or form delivery
  • Independent providers or provider networks connected to the request flow
  • Legal or regulatory parties where disclosure is required by law

PipeMatch does not promise that every request will result in contact, a quote, or a provider match.

Cookies & Tracking

PipeMatch may use cookies, local storage, analytics tools, and similar technologies to support site functionality, remember certain preferences, measure usage, and improve the browsing experience.

Some tools may help understand which pages users visit, how they interact with the site, whether they submit forms, and how site performance can be improved.

Users can review our separate Cookie Policy for more detail about how cookie-related tools may be used on the site.

Data Retention

PipeMatch may retain personal and technical information for as long as reasonably necessary to operate the site, support legitimate business purposes, maintain records, resolve disputes, or comply with legal obligations.

Retention periods may vary depending on the type of data involved, the purpose for which it was collected, and any legal or operational requirements that apply.

Your Choices

Users may choose not to provide certain personal information, although doing so may limit access to some features, form submissions, or request-related functions.

Depending on applicable law, users may also have rights related to access, correction, deletion, or limitation of certain personal information. Requests may be evaluated based on the nature of the information and any legal or operational requirements involved.

Users may also adjust browser settings to manage cookie behavior, although disabling some cookies may affect site functionality.
